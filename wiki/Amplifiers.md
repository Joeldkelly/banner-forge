## DM template for Tier 1

Short. Personal. No ask. Link to private preview.

Example for [[Pietro Schirano]]:

> Hey Pietro — been following your work since Claude Engineer. I just built a Claude skill that turns one Figma frame into 60 validated banner zips (Google/DV360/TTD/Adform) in under a minute. Open-sourcing it in ~5 days.
>
> Quick preview: [private video link]
> Repo goes public Tuesday 8:30am PT. No ask — just wanted to get it in front of you first.

## Why this ordering matters

- **Tier 1 first** seeds the early retweet graph on launch day. Pietro's RT alone can outweigh all other channels combined.
- **Tier 3 newsletters** have 7-day lead times for their editorial calendar.
- **Awesome-list PRs** compound over weeks — they're not launch-day, they're launch-week and beyond.
- **Product Hunt 2–3 weeks later** gives you a fresh audience and a second wave of social proof (GitHub stars from week 1 feed into PH's "hot" algorithm).
