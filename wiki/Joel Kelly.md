# Joel Kelly

The author and maintainer of banner-forge.

## Role

- Senior digital designer. ~15 years shipping banner campaigns at corporate shops.
- Most recently: Dyson, North America digital designer.
- Independent consultant at [[joel.design]] — designing, building, and shipping web + AI tooling for agencies and in-house creative teams.

## Why Joel is the right person to ship banner-forge

- Direct first-hand experience with "the resizing day" — the pain the skill replaces.
- 15 years of ad-ops, trafficking, and network-specific quirk knowledge. The clickTag dual-global pattern, the Adform manifest, the Amazon retina requirement, the lottiefiles.com CORS rejection — all from muscle memory.
- Designer-first perspective, not dev-first. The Claude skill ecosystem as of 2026 is dev-heavy; design skills are rare and disproportionately amplified (see [[Amplifiers]]).
- Personal-brand playbook mindset. joel.design is the funnel, banner-forge is the portfolio piece. See [[Consulting Funnel]].
